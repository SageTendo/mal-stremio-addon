MAL-Stremio Addon
{% endblock %} {% block content %}

Access and manage your MyAnimeList (MAL) anime lists directly in Stremio. Easily track your watched anime, plan to watch, and sync progress with MAL automatically.

Features

  • View and manage your MAL anime lists
  • Filter anime by genre
  • Sync progress (Watching, Completed, On Hold) with MAL

How Syncing Works

  • Watching from "Plan to Watch" → Moves to "Watching"
  • Movies from "Plan to Watch" → Moves to "Completed"
  • Watching from "Watching" → Watched; last episode marks "Completed"
  • Watching from "On Hold" → Moves to "Watching"
Note: Since Stremio lacks built-in tracking, the addon marks progress when subtitles are requested, not when the episode is fully watched.
No Content Showing?
If no content is being displayed, it could be the result of your session expiring. Log in again to MyAnimeList or 'Refresh MAL' on the addon website.
